VJ VIK MIL is an audiovisual artist & composer whose work explores the intersection of music, digital art, and technology. Active since 2008, his tracks have been played by renowned DJs worldwide.

In 2022, he launched the VJ VIK MIL project to push creative boundaries through collaborations with emerging digital artists in the Web3 space. His work elevates digital artworks into fully immersive experiences where sound and visuals become inseparable.

In July 2024, he presented his first AI-driven collaboration, which was featured in Fellowship’s Daily program, a leading platform for contemporary digital art.

In 2025, he released data_transform, a conceptual video series that reached a wide online audience. The project explores how technology distorts and abstracts human experience, reducing identity and presence into data streams and algorithmic patterns.
