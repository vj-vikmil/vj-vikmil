About Me

I am VJ VIK MIL, an audiovisual artist and composer whose work explores the intersection of music, digital art, and technology. My projects combine sound and visuals into immersive experiences that challenge conventional boundaries of performance and design.

Over the years, I’ve collaborated with digital creators, experimented with glitch aesthetics, and explored how algorithms reshape human perception. My goal is to craft audiovisual journeys that live at the edge of chaos and beauty.
