VJ VIK MIL

Viktors Mileika is a London-based multimedia artist, video engineer, and VJ. An alumnus of the Guildhall School of Music & Drama, where he studied Video Design for Live Performance, he now also teaches video design and projection mapping there.

He has created live visuals for Brockwell Live festivals (Mighty Hoopla, City Splash, Wide Awake, Cross The Tracks), Fuse London at Sonar Festival in Barcelona, the Sands Festival in Margate, and the Ibiza Orchestra Tour.

Always curious and exploring new possibilities, Viktors uses tools like Notch and TouchDesigner to build immersive, responsive visuals that bring energy and atmosphere to every stage.
